Welcome!

This website is about Blockbench, a 3D modeling application that is used primarily by Mojang to create models for the game Minecraft.

Recently, it's been a personal hobby of mine to create models using Blockbench for fun! This site will include the following:

  • A concise explanation of Blockbench and where to download it.
  • A short tutorial on how to get started making a simple entity model!
  • A few examples of models I've made in the past few months.
  • A contact form in case you have questions for me.
I personally hope you enjoy what my site has to offer, and if you've ever had an interest in 3D modeling, but are too intimidated by stuff like Blender or Maya, I encourage you to give Blockbench a try. Its free!
